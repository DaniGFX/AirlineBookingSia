Our Service Guarantee

At FlightFinder, we're committed to providing exceptional service. Our guarantee ensures that you receive the best possible experience from booking to landing.

Best Price Guarantee

Find a lower price for the same flight within 24 hours of booking? We'll refund the difference and give you a $50 travel voucher for your next trip.

Flight Disruption Protection

If your flight is cancelled or delayed by more than 3 hours, our team will work to find you an alternative flight at no additional cost. If no suitable alternative is available, we'll provide a full refund.

24/7 Customer Support

Our dedicated support team is available around the clock to assist with any issues or questions you may have before, during, or after your journey.
