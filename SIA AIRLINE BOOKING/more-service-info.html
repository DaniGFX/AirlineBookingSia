Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response when I contact customer support?

Our standard response time is within 30 minutes for chat and email inquiries, and under 2 minutes for phone calls. Premium members receive priority handling with even faster response times.

What happens if I need to change or cancel my flight?

Our team will guide you through the change or cancellation process, explaining any applicable fees and helping you find alternatives if needed. We'll work to minimize any penalties based on the airline's policies.

Can you help with special requests like seat selection or dietary requirements?

Yes, we can assist with all special requests including seat preferences, meal requirements, accessibility needs, and more. Just let us know your requirements when booking or contact our support team.

Is there an additional cost for using your customer support services?

Basic support is included with all bookings at no extra charge. Premium support services are available as optional upgrades or included with certain membership tiers.
