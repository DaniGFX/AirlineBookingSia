Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a flight?

To book a flight, simply fill in the search form with your departure and arrival locations, preferred travel dates, and passenger details. Click "Search Flights" to view available options, select your preferred flight, and follow the checkout process to complete your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payment methods via credit card

Can I change or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can change or cancel your booking subject to the fare rules of your ticket. Most tickets allow changes with a fee, while cancellation policies vary by fare type. To make changes, log into your account or contact our customer service team.

How can I check the status of my flight?

You can check your flight status by entering your flight number or route details in the "Flight Status" section of our website or mobile app. We also send real-time notifications about delays or changes to the email and phone number provided during booking.

What is your baggage policy?

Baggage allowances vary by airline, route, and fare class. Generally, economy tickets include one carry-on bag and one checked bag up to 23kg. Premium cabins offer increased allowances. Specific details will be displayed during the booking process and in your confirmation email.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance options that cover trip cancellations, medical emergencies, lost baggage, and travel delays. You can add insurance during the booking process or purchase it separately from your account dashboard.

How do I contact customer support?

Our customer support team is available 24/7. You can reach us through live chat on our website, by email at support@flightfinder.com, or by phone at 1-800-FLIGHTS. For urgent inquiries related to same-day travel, we recommend calling our priority helpline.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and payment information. We never share your data with third parties without your consent, and we comply with global data protection regulations including GDPR.
