Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FlightFinder collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our services.

Last Updated: May 7, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us, such as your name, email address, payment details, and booking preferences. We may also collect technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your flight bookings and payments
  • To provide customer service and support
  • To send booking confirmations and updates
  • To improve our services and website experience

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • Airlines and travel partners to fulfill your bookings
  • Payment processors to complete transactions
  • Legal authorities, if required by law

4. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure, and we encourage you to protect your login credentials.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze site traffic and improve user experience. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent to marketing communications
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or a notice on our website.
